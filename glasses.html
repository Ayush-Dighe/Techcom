Objective - We have designed glasses in such a manner that when a driver falls asleep, the buzzer activates and the wheel symbolizes the vehicle stops which helps to prevent accidents.

Materials used Arduino Uno, Jumper Wires, Buzzer, Battery, Relay Module, IR Sensor, Googles , DG Motor , Wheels.
