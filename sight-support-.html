Objective - This project can be used by Visually Impaired People in navigating around their vicinity. It gives everybody hope that they can be visually paired with technology to explore the new world together at the same pace and constant efforts are being made from our group in bringing this project to life.

Materials used Set of wheels, DC Motor, Magnetic slate, Wires, Arduino R3, Batteries.
